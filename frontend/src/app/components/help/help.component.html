We supply all sorts of both ingredients and consumables, and are working on improving our supply!
Please not we do not take any responsibility for reactions or complications arising from administration of our products.
Please note the life effect markings and tags on our products! Life effect scales from -5 to +5, and things labelled as an ingredients are dangerous to consume unless also marked as consumable.

ORDERING

We operate by mail order! You don't need to worry about entering any address or payment info, as we will definitely find you wherever you need.
Customers can typically expect deliveries within 2-4 minutes. Just make sure to leave a window open!
Place your order by printing and shredding your cart page. Drop the pieces out of your window and your order will be on its way!

BREAKING THE 4TH WALL

What is this?

This is a practical exercise in developing with Angular! Express handles the backend and works with Sequelize.js API for the PostgreSQL database to connect with the angular components Also, everything was designed with TailwindCSS.
